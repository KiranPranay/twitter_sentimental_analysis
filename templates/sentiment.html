About SentiTrend

Sentitrend is a sentiment analysis tool that harnesses the power of machine learning and natural language processing to analyze the sentiment of tweets on a particular topic. Our tool provides insights into the positive, negative, and neutral sentiments associated with any given keyword or hashtag, allowing you to gain valuable insights into public opinion and trends. With Sentitrend, you can stay on top of the latest trends and conversations on Twitter, and make data-driven decisions based on real-time sentiment analysis.
